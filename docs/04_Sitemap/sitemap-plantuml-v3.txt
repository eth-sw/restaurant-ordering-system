@startmindmap
* A Web-Based Restaurant Ordering System

' RIGHT SIDE (Customers
** Guests (Public)
*** Home Page (/)\n(Home/View Menu and Check Delivery)
*** Checkout (/checkout)\n(View Basket and Stripe Payment)
*** Login (/login)\n(User Login)
*** Register (/register)\n(Customer Account Creation)

** Customer (Protected)
*** Home Page (/)\n(Home/View Menu and Check Delivery)
*** Checkout (/checkout)\n(View Basket and Stripe Payment)
*** Order History (/orders)\n(View Personal Order History)
*** Profile (/profile)\n(Edit Account Details)
*** Logout (/logout)\n(Ends User Session)

' LEFT SIDE (Management
left side

** Staff (Protected)
*** Kitchen (/kitchen)\n(View Active Orders, Update Order Status)

** Supervisor (Protected)
*** Add Menu Item (/add-menu)\n(Form to Add New Menu Items)
*** Edit/Delete Menu Items (/edit-menu/:id)\n(Form to Modify or Delete Items)
*** Sales (/reports/sales)\n(View Revenue and Popular Items)
*** Refunds (/orders/refunds)\n(Process Order Cancellations)
*** Store Status (/store-status)\n(Manually Open/Close the Restaurant)

** Admin (Protected)
*** Delivery Zone (/delivery-zone)\n(Draw/Update Google Maps Geofence)
*** User Management (/admin/users)\n(Create Accounts, Reset Passwords)
*** Settings (/admin/settings)\n(Update Delivery Fees, Business Address)
*** Integrations (/admin/integrations)\n(Manage Stripe, Maps, Twilio Keys)
*** Logs (/admin/logs)\n(View System Error Logs and Audit Trails)
@endmindmap
