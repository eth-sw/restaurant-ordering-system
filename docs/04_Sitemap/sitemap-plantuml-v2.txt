@startwbs
* A Web-Based Restaurant Ordering System
** Public (Guests)
*** Home Page (/)\n(Home/View Menu and Check Delivery)
*** Checkout (/checkout)\n(View Basket and Stripe Payment)
*** Login (/login)\n(User Login)
*** Register (/register)\n(Customer Account Creation)
** Customer (Protected)
*** Home Page (/)\n(Home/View Menu and Check Delivery)
*** Checkout (/checkout)\n(View Basket and Stripe Payment)
*** Order History (/orders)\n(View Personal Order History)
*** Profile (/profile)\n(Edit Account Details)
** Staff (Protected)
*** Kitchen (/kitchen)\n(View Active Orders, Update Order Status)
** Supervisor (Protected)
*** Add Menu Item (/add-menu)\n(Form to Add New Menu Items)
*** Edit/Delete Menu Items (/edit-menu/:id)\n(Form to Modify or Delete Menu Items)
*** Sales (/reports/sales)\n(View Daily and Weekly Revenue, and Popular Items)
*** Refunds (/orders/refunds)\n(Process Order Cancellations and Stripe Refunds)
*** Store Status (/store-status)\n(Manually Open/Close the Restaurant)
** Admin (Protected)
*** Delivery Zone (/delivery-zone)\n(Draw/Update Google Maps Polygon Geofence)
*** User Management (/admin/users)\n(Create Accounts, Reset Passwords, etc.)
*** Settings (/admin/settings)\n(Update Delivery Fees, or Business Address, etc.)
*** Integrations (/admin/integrations)\n(Manage API Keys for Stripe, Google Maps, and Twilio)
*** Logs (/admin/logs)\n(View System Error Logs and Audit Trails)
** Global Actions
*** Logout (/logout)\n(Ends User Session)
@endwbs
